National Aeronautics and Space Administration

History

NASA was established in 1958, succeeding the National Advisory Committee for Aeronautics (NACA). Since then, NASA has led American space exploration efforts, including the Apollo Moon landing missions, the Skylab space station, and the Space Shuttle program.

Mission

NASA's mission is to drive advances in science, technology, aeronautics, and space exploration to enhance knowledge, education, innovation, economic vitality, and stewardship of Earth.

Key Programs

  • Artemis: Return humans to the Moon
  • Mars Exploration: Rovers and future human missions
  • ISS: International Space Station research
  • James Webb: Next-generation space telescope
  • Earth Science: Climate and environmental monitoring

Achievements

  • First humans on the Moon (Apollo 11, 1969)
  • Mars rovers: Spirit, Opportunity, Curiosity, Perseverance
  • Hubble Space Telescope discoveries
  • International Space Station construction
  • Commercial crew program partnerships

What is Space Apps?

NASA's International Space Apps Challenge is an annual hackathon that takes place over 48 hours in cities around the world. The event embraces collaborative problem solving with a goal of producing relevant open-source solutions to address global needs applicable to both life on Earth and life in space.

Our Challenge

"Explore Massive Datasets Visually"

Objective: Create a platform that allows users to zoom in and out of NASA's massive image datasets, label known features, and discover new patterns.

Our Solution: STAR Explorer

STAR Explorer (Space Telescope Analysis & Research) is our innovative solution that provides:

  • Deep Zoom Technology: Explore massive images at multiple scales
  • Multi-Dataset Support: Earth observations, lunar maps, planetary imagery
  • Comparison Tools: Side-by-side analysis of temporal and spectral data
  • Feature Labeling: Collaborative annotation system
  • Public Gallery: Museum-mode for educational outreach
  • Researcher Tools: Advanced analysis capabilities
